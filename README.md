# cryptoAGI

**Cryptocurrency Autonomous General Intelligence.**

cryptoAGI is an open effort to build intelligence that is native to open networks — software that
can reason, act, and sustain itself using the same rails people use to hold and move value, without
asking anyone's permission. The premise is simple: an autonomous system should own its own keys,
run on infrastructure anyone can inspect, and answer to its user rather than a platform.

## What we're building toward

- **Self-sovereign by default.** Keys are generated and held by the user, not by us. Systems are
  designed to be *non-custodial* — they can help you hold, verify, and move value, but they are
  structurally unable to spend on your behalf. Your coins stay yours.
- **Local-first.** The things that matter — keys, models, decisions — should be able to run on your
  own machine. No mandatory cloud, no phone-home, no lock-in.
- **Open where it counts.** The code that guards a user's keys and the tooling others build on is
  meant to be free and auditable. Trust should come from being able to read the code, not from a
  brand.
- **Native to the network.** Prices, settlement, and coordination come from the chains themselves,
  not from an opaque middle layer. When something can be verified on-chain, it should be.

## The shape of the work

cryptoAGI spans a few layers that reinforce each other:

- **Autonomous intelligence** — agents and models that can operate, learn, and improve over time.
- **Open AI infrastructure** — efficient model serving and multimodal tooling that runs on
  commodity hardware.
- **Self-custody & identity** — wallets and wallet-signature authentication, so an agent (or a
  person) proves who they are with a key instead of a password.
- **On-chain finance** — protocols and automation built directly on public blockchains.

The unifying idea: an intelligent system that can fund and govern itself through open financial
rails, custody value without a custodian, and run on infrastructure its user controls.

## Open building blocks

A few of our efforts are public today and give a sense of the direction:

- **OpenMIND** — a native, local-first AI workspace (multi-model, runs against your own models).
- **Self-evolution research** — methods for systems that refine their own procedures over time.
- **Open AI infrastructure** — high-throughput model inference and generation tooling.
- **Wallet-signature authentication** — logging in and proving identity with a crypto key.
- **Network discovery & market tooling** — public utilities for exploring blockchain networks and
  market data.

These sit alongside the broader ecosystem cryptoAGI participates in — including open,
**non-custodial Bitcoin tooling** built to the *BTC Standard* (client-side keys, your own node) —
and a body of applied work that matures in the open as it's ready.

## Principles we won't trade away

- **Non-custodial, always.** We never hold your keys or your seed.
- **No telemetry, no surveillance.** Local-first means what it says.
- **Reversible and honest.** Tools do what they claim, and every safeguard has an off switch.
- **User over platform.** The system protects *you*; it never locks you out of your own assets.
