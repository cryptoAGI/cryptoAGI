## Who we are

We are **cryptoAGI**. We build intelligence that is native to open networks — systems that can
reason, decide, and act using the same rails people use to hold and move value, without asking
anyone's permission.

Most "AI" today rents you access to a model that lives on someone else's computer, learns from
your data, and can be switched off at will. We think autonomy should mean something stronger: a
system that **holds its own keys, runs on infrastructure anyone can inspect, and answers to its
user — not to a platform.** That is the whole idea behind *cryptocurrency autonomous general
intelligence*: intelligence with agency over open money and open compute, and no custodian in the
middle.

## What we believe

- **Self-sovereign by default.** Keys are generated and held by the user, never by us. What we
  build is *non-custodial* — it can help you hold, verify, and move value, but it is structurally
  unable to spend on your behalf. Your coins stay yours.
- **Local-first.** The things that matter — keys, models, decisions — should run on hardware you
  control. No mandatory cloud, no phone-home, no lock-in.
- **Open where it counts.** The code that guards a user's keys, and the tooling others build on,
  should be free and auditable. Trust is earned by being readable, not by a logo.
- **Native to the network.** Prices, settlement, and coordination come from the chains themselves,
  not from an opaque middle layer. If it can be verified on-chain, it should be.
- **The user over the platform.** Every system we ship protects *you*, and never locks you out of
  your own assets. Every safeguard has an off switch.

## What we build

cryptoAGI spans a few layers that reinforce one another — an intelligence that can fund and govern
itself through open financial rails, custody value without a custodian, and run on infrastructure
its user controls:

- **Autonomous intelligence** — agents and models that operate, learn, and refine their own
  procedures over time.
- **Open AI infrastructure** — efficient, high-throughput model serving and multimodal tooling
  that runs on commodity hardware, locally.
- **Self-custody & identity** — wallets and wallet-signature authentication, so a person or an
  agent proves who they are with a key instead of a password.
- **On-chain finance** — automation and protocols built directly on public blockchains, where
  settlement is native and verifiable.

## Open building blocks

Some of our work is public today and shows the direction of travel:

- **OpenMIND** — a native, local-first AI workspace: multi-model, run against your own models, on
  your own machine.
- **Self-evolution research** — methods for systems that improve their own procedures over time.
- **Open inference infrastructure** — high-throughput model serving and generation tooling.
- **Wallet-signature authentication** — logging in and proving identity with a cryptographic key.
- **Network discovery & market tooling** — public utilities for exploring blockchain networks and
  reading markets.

These mature in the open as they are ready. Much of the deeper work is developed privately and
revealed alongside the vision, in its own time.
